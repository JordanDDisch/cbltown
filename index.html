Home - Campbelltown City Council

Campbelltown City, NSW, Australia



Welcome to the City of Campbelltown - the capital of the Macarthur region.

Located just 50 minutes from the Sydney CBD and within easy reach of major road and rail links to the Southern Highlands, Canberra, the Blue Mountains and the South Coast beaches, Campbelltown offers a unique combination of city opportunities in a natural setting to its 150,000 residents.

If you live in the area, are visiting or planning a trip, there’s plenty to see and do.

Campbelltown City Council acknowledges that the City of Campbelltown is located on the traditional lands of the Dharawal people.
